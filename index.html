About me

My name is Wesley Willekes. I am a medical student with a personal interest in computer science. I 100% taught myself to code, and didn't spend a penny whilst doing so. Lately I have been doing a lot of web development, and I decided it would be a cool idea to create a showcase of the results. I hope that by making my project open source I can help people, like so many open source projects have done for me. This also opens up the posibility of other people collaborating and helping me improve my projects, which is an exciting thought. Finally I hope some people can find some joy in playing around with them. I am looking forward to collaborating, receiving feedback and most importantly: having fun.
